How to make a sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 slices of deli meat (turkey, ham, etc.)
  • 1 slice of cheese (cheddar, Swiss, etc.)
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Mayonnaise or mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Lay out the two slices of bread on a clean surface.
  2. Spread mayonnaise or mustard on one side of each slice of bread.
  3. Place the slices of deli meat on one slice of bread.
  4. Add the slice of cheese on top of the meat.
  5. Layer the lettuce leaves and tomato slices over the cheese.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Top with the second slice of bread, mayonnaise or mustard side down.
  8. Press down gently to hold the sandwich together.
  9. Cut the sandwich in half if desired, and serve immediately.

Informations:

Preparation time:
10 minutes
Servings:
1 person
Difficulty:
Easy
